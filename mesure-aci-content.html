ACI Measures & Objectives

ACI Measure ( Protect Patient Health Information )

Protect electronic protected health information (ePHI) created or maintained by the certified EHR technology through the implementation of appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards.

Security Risk Analysis Measure:
