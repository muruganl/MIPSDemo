Definition

What is MIPS?

  • A new system of reimbursement for Medicare providers
  • Consolidates Meaningful Use Program, Physician Quality Reporting System and Value-Based Modifier
  • Begins in 2019
  • Payment to providers will be based on composite score for four factors
    • Quality 50%
    • Resource Use 10%
    • Clinical Practice Improvement 15%
    • Advance Care Information 25%
