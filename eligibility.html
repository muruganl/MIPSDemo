Eligibility

Years 1 and 2

Physicians (MD/DO and DMD/DDS), PAs, NPs, Clinical nurse specialists, Certified registered nurse anesthetists.

Year 3+

Physical or Occupational therapists, Speech-language pathologists, Audiologists, Nurse midwives, Clinical social workers, Clinical psychologists, Dietitians/Nutritional professionals.

There are 3 groups of clinicians who will NOT be subject to MIPS:

First year of Medicare PART B participation

Below low patient volume threshold (Medicare billing charges less than or equal to $10,000 and provides care for 100 or fewer Medicare patients in one year)

Certain Participants in ADVANCED Alternative payment Models
